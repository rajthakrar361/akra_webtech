What is Co-Win

Co-WIN is a platform for the citizens of India to Register for COVID-19 vaccination and schedule their vaccination slots at the nearest vaccination centers.

Progress and Statistics

Currently 13 crore of the indian population has already been vaccinated. That totals around 10% of the indian population! India is the fastest country to do so too!
